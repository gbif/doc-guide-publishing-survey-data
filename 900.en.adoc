== Additional guidance and reaching out for assistance

Need more information? Check out the following documentation:

* Humboldt extension for ecological inventories
(https://eco.tdwg.org/) +
* GBIF Technical Documents (https://techdocs.gbif.org/en/) +
* The OBIS Manual (https://manual.obis.org/)

Or, reach out for assistance from:

* https://github.com/tdwg/hc[Humboldt Extension GitHub repository]:
questions about usage, issues with the vocabulary, and recommendations
for new terms should be reported as an Issue. +
* GBIF community forum on Discourse (https://discourse.gbif.org/) +
* The GBIF Node for your country or organization
** If your country or organization is a member of GBIF and has an
established node, you can read out directly to your node.
*** If you’re uncertain if your country or organization is part of the
GBIF network you can https://www.gbif.org/the-gbif-network[search
here]. +
** If your country or organization is not a member of GBIF, reach out to
the GBIF help desk (helpdesk@gbif.org) +
* GBIF help desk
** create an issue on the GitHub tech-docs project
(https://techdocs.gbif.org/en/) +
** or, send an email (helpdesk@gbif.org)
